• Welcome to Pixelsior!
  • Pixelsior is a simple tool to compare how well your html+css matches the design.
  • Here is a simple guide on how to use it:
  • First, open your design, make sure the scale is 100%. Take a screenshot of the element you are interested in and drag it to this page.
  • Next, open your html+css, scale the page to 100%, take a screenshot of the same element and drag it here.
  • Now that there are 2 images on this page, just drag the second one on top of the first!
  • The better the pixels match each other in terms of color, the blacker it will be.
  • The greater the difference, the brighter the color will be!
  • Let's go!
